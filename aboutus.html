About Us

Afri-Global is a digital platform built by seven young Ghanian girls, created to inspire and uplift upcoming musicians,content creators and fashion designers - as well as other talents.

Launched on September 16th, 2027, we started with 25 users and 30 commission providers.Thanks to advertisers using our platform,we earned our very first monthly profit of 26,000 Ghana cedis.

Our mission is to give visibility to upcoming musicians,producers,fashion designers and content creators.
